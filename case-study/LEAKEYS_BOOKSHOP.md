# Case Study: Leakey's Bookshop

## Business Profile

**Leakey's Bookshop** is a real, beloved independent bookstore located in Inverness, Scotland. It serves as the central case study for this workshop, providing a rich, authentic context for applying prompt engineering techniques to real-world marketing challenges.

| Attribute | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Leakey's Bookshop |
| **Location** | Church Street, Inverness, Scottish Highlands |
| **Founded** | 1979 by Charles Leakey |
| **Current Location** | A former Gaelic church (since 1994) |
| **Size** | Scotland's largest secondhand bookshop |
| **Collection** | Over 100,000 books, maps, and prints |
| **Specialties** | Secondhand and antiquarian books, maps, prints |

### Unique Features

The bookstore is renowned for its unique and evocative atmosphere, which is a key element to capture in marketing communications.

*   **Architectural Setting:** Housed in a historic Gaelic church, complete with stained-glass windows and a pulpit.
*   **Cozy Atmosphere:** A large wood-burning stove (log fire) creates a warm and inviting environment, especially during the colder months.
*   **Vast Collection:** The sheer volume of books, stacked high on shelves that reach the ceiling, creates a sense of wonder and discovery.
*   **Iconic Spiral Staircase:** A cast-iron spiral staircase connects the two floors and is a frequently photographed feature.
*   **45+ Years of History:** The business has a long-standing reputation and is a cultural landmark in Inverness.

### Target Audience

Leakey's appeals to a diverse range of customers, which presents both an opportunity and a challenge for targeted marketing.

1.  **Local Bibliophiles:** Residents of Inverness and the surrounding Highlands who are regular customers and value the store's community feel.
2.  **Literary Tourists:** Visitors to Scotland who are book lovers and seek out unique, iconic bookstores as part of their travel experience.
3.  **Rare Book Collectors:** Serious collectors looking for antiquarian books, first editions, and rare prints.
4.  **Casual Browsers:** Tourists and locals who stumble upon the shop and are drawn in by its unique appearance and atmosphere.

### Marketing Goal for this Session

Your goal as a marketing consultant for Leakey's is to create a cohesive set of foundational marketing assets that:

1.  **Capture the unique character** and atmosphere of the bookstore.
2.  **Attract both local and tourist audiences** with targeted messaging.
3.  **Are optimized for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**, meaning they are structured to be easily understood, trusted, and presented by AI-powered answer engines (like Google SGE, Perplexity) in response to relevant local queries.
